The Course

This 7,200 yard 72 par golf course designed by Brooks Koepka features fresh Hawaiian air, immaculately designed greens, and beautiful Hawaiian Vistas.

Wanika offers world class golf unlike anything you can find on the islands. The cool island breeze is perfect for golf anytime of year.
