The luxurious Wanika Spa has been Zagat-rated as a top destination on the island. The spa features:

  • Full Body Massages: Whether you're looking to unknot muscles after a vigorous hike, detoxify the body, or simply surrender to a state of pure bliss, one of our skilled massage therapists will leave you feeling nothing short of fantastic.
  • Facials: Being the body’s largest and most vulnerable organ, your skin demands constant consideration. Wanika offers a full array of treatments to counter the effects of the sun, environmental toxins, premature aging and specific skin conditions.
  • Soaks: The spa is equipped with body a sauna and ice tubs for soaking that facilitate a relaxation of the mind and body.
